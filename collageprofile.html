About the College

Government Engineering College, San Francisco, is a prestigious institution known for its academic excellence and vibrant campus life. Established in 1965, the college offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, technology, and applied sciences. The college is committed to providing quality education and fostering research and innovation.

Programs Offered

  • Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Technology in Electrical Engineering
  • Bachelor of Technology in Mechanical Engineering
  • Master of Technology in Data Science
  • Master of Technology in Cybersecurity

Facilities

  • State-of-the-art laboratories and research centers
  • Library with over 50,000 books and digital resources
  • Modern sports complex and gymnasium
  • Hostel accommodations for students
  • On-campus medical facilities
